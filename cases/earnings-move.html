Case Study: How Apple Earnings Moved the Market

On July 30, 2020, Apple (AAPL) reported record earnings after the market closed. The next morning, AAPL gapped up 10% at the open, leading to a surge in tech stocks and the NASDAQ index. Day traders who anticipated the move by analyzing pre-market volume and news were able to capitalize on the volatility.

  • Lesson: Earnings reports can create large gaps and volatility. Always check the economic calendar and pre-market news before trading.
  • Strategy: Use pre-market volume and Level II data to gauge interest and potential direction.
  • Risk: Gaps can reverse quickly; use tight stops and avoid chasing price.
