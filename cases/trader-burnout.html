Case Study: How Burnout Ruined a Profitable Trader

Alex was a consistently profitable day trader for two years. In 2022, he started trading longer hours, skipping breaks, and obsessing over every tick. His performance declined, and he began making impulsive trades. Eventually, Alex lost a significant portion of his account.

  • Lesson: Mental health and work-life balance are critical for trading success.
  • Strategy: Schedule regular breaks, set daily time limits, and practice mindfulness.
  • Risk: Burnout leads to poor decision-making and increased risk of large losses.
