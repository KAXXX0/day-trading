Case Study: Joining a Prop Firm

Maria wanted to scale her trading but lacked capital. She joined a proprietary trading firm, passed their evaluation, and received $100,000 in buying power. She kept 80% of profits and paid a small monthly desk fee.

  • Lesson: Prop firms can provide leverage and resources for skilled traders.
  • Strategy: Focus on risk management and consistency to pass evaluations.
  • Risk: High leverage increases risk; desk fees and profit splits reduce net income.
